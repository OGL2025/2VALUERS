WHO WE ARE

Vision & Background

2M Valuers & Property Consultants is a 100% nationally owned Valuation and Property Consultancy service business based in Port Moresby. We also have dedicated agents stationed in Lae and Goroka to efficiently handle assignments across those regions.

The business commenced operations in mid-August 2024 and has grown steadily ever since. Our team is led by our Director/Principal Valuer, supported by two (2) Graduate Valuers who have been with the firm since its inception, consistently delivering highly competent and reliable service across all responsibilities and assignments.

Our Commitment

Competence: Led by a Principal Valuer with dedicated graduate professionals ensuring reliable service.

National Reach: Strategically positioned in Port Moresby, Lae, and Goroka to serve you better.
