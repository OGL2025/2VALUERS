WHO WE ARE

Vision & Background

2M Valuers and Properties Consultants is an independent chartered valuation practice established in 2009. We serve residential, commercial, and agricultural mandates across the East African region.

We deliberately stay small — ten valuers, three support staff — so that every report carries the name of the partner who signed it. We do not franchise. We do not sub-contract. Every valuation is conducted by a senior valuer who personally inspects the subject property.

Our Charter

Independence: We do not act for both sides of a transaction.

Evidence: Every valuation is grounded in verified comparable evidence.

Core Values
