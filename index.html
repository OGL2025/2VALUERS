WHO WE ARE

Your Trusted Property Advisors

2M Valuers & Property Consultants

2M Valuers & Property Consultants is a 100% nationally owned Valuation and Property Consultancy service business based in Port Moresby. We also have dedicated agents stationed in Lae and Goroka to efficiently handle assignments across those regions.

The business commenced operations in mid-August 2024 and has grown steadily ever since. Our team is led by our Director/Principal Valuer, supported by two (2) Graduate Valuers who have been with the firm since its inception, consistently delivering highly competent and reliable service.
