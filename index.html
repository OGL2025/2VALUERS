WHO WE ARE

Your Trusted Property Advisors

2M Valuers and Properties Consultants

2M Valuers is an independent chartered valuation practice serving residential, commercial, and agricultural mandates. Every report we sign is grounded in comparable evidence and local market intelligence.

Established in 2009, we deliberately stay small so that every report carries the name of the partner who signed it. We do not franchise. We do not sub-contract.
